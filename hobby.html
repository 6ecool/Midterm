Russian
Billiard

Russian billiards, or "Russian pyramid," is a cue sport played on a 12-foot table without pockets, using 16 balls: a cue ball and 15 object balls. Players score points by legally pocketing balls, with various rule sets for different game variants, such as "dynamic pyramid" and "free pyramid." Popular in Russia, the sport emphasizes precision and strategy and has gained international recognition through tournaments and professional play.

Table
Tennis

Table tennis, or ping pong, is a fast-paced indoor sport played on a table divided by a net. In singles or doubles, players use paddles to hit a lightweight ball, aiming to score points by making the opponent fail to return it. Matches typically go to 11 points, with a two-point lead needed to win. Governed by the ITTF, the sport emphasizes speed and strategy and is enjoyed globally at recreational and competitive levels.

Brawl
Stars

Brawl Stars is a multiplayer online battle game by Supercell where players choose unique "Brawlers" to compete in various modes, such as Gem Grab and Showdown. Matches last a few minutes and emphasize quick strategy and teamwork. With colorful graphics and regular updates, the game has gained a large global player base, known for its competitive gameplay and engaging community.
